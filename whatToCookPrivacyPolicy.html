Privacy Policy


What To Cook


Data Collection


We do not have access to any data that users store within their app. We request permission to access the user's photos in order to enable users to attach an image to their recipes. These images are not able to be viewed by us in any form and access to your photos can be changed at any time from within your device's settings. Furthermore we do not share personal data with any third party companies. Finally, if you wish for data to be deleted, simply delete the record from your app and it will be removed from the wider system. No data is stored for longer then it is used.
